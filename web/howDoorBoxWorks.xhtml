The DoorBox gives you the freedom to shop online without the hassle of being present to sign for your package. Receiving a Parcel

It’s simple!  When expecting a package, just leave your DoorBox unlocked.  Place our specially prepared courier instructions on your front door, or on the DoorBox itself. Couriers recognizing the DoorBox will simply lift the lid and place your parcel inside.  The courier records your unique code located on the underside of the DoorBox lid as Proof-of-Signature. The courier then simply turns the lever to lock the DoorBox and secure your package. When you get home, enter your 4 digit access code to retrieve your package.

Leaving a Parcel for Pick-up

Need to leave a parcel for a courier, a customer or friend to pick-up when you are not at home?  Simply place the parcel in the DoorBox and lock it.  Then using our convenient web portal or mobile application, create a temporary code for a date and time in the future, select how long the code is to remain active, then send the code to whoever is picking up the parcel via email or SMS.

When they arrive, they simply punch in the code from their email or SMS message to open the DoorBox and retrieve your package.

Installing the DoorBox

Don't have time? Not handy with tools?  No worries. For only $39.99 including delivery of the DoorBox itself, we will dispatch one of our friendly installers to come right to your front door and install the DoorBox for you.

Want to do it yourself?  We can ship the DoorBox to you.  When you receive your DoorBox, place it in front of your home easily visible to couriers.  The DoorBox comes with mounting holes and a full installation kit to allow you to securely fasten the DoorBox to a foundation or wall.
