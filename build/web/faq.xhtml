How is the DoorBox powered?

The DoorBox lock requires two standard AAA batteries to operate.  These batteries are already installed and your lock initialized when you buy your DoorBox.  Your batteries will provide you approximately 15,000 openings.

Can the DoorBox cope with multiple deliveries?

Yes, this is one of DoorBox’s unique features. The DoorBox lock uses a code entry system, meaning that whoever has the code can deliver parcels securely to your DoorBox time after time.

Can you install the DoorBox outside?

Yes, the DoorBox has been designed and manufactured specifically for outside use. Using a pre-coating process on a steel substrate, the DoorBox has then been further treated and painted to withstand outdoor weather.

What happens if the parcel needs a signature?

Each DoorBox has a unique number situated inside the lid and very visible to couriers that acts as a proof of signature. Couriers prefer to deliver their parcels the first time and therefore the unique number under the DoorBox lid is becoming more widely accepted as proof of delivery. However there may be the occasional delivery that requires a hand written signature.

How do I install the DoorBox?

Actually, you have two installation options:

If you want to install the DoorBox yourself, it is quite simple: Each DoorBox comes 4 mounting holes in the base and 4 in the back of the unit.  We recommend that the DoorBox is bolted (using bolts supplied) to a wall or foundation. To see how installation works, review the Operations Manual found in the “How DoorBox Works” section.

Your other more convenient option, if you live in the GTA, is to have us install it for you. For practically the same cost to ship the DoorBox, one of our friendly installers will make an appointment with you and install it in a place of your choosing. No need to worry about tools, and the DoorBox will be installed and activated in no time.
